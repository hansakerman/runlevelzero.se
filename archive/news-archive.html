Release dates for the new album

9:35 pm, dec 29 2007

It's finally official - at the end of january our third studio album, Arctic Noise, will be released world wide.
The album contains 12 tracks, with additional production courtesy of Oscar Holter of Necro Facility and Chris P.

This is the tracklisting:

   01. Black Cinder
   02. Deny Me
   03. Hitting Ground
   04. Stroke
   05. Incision
   06. With One Voice
   07. Shine
   08. Hand To Mouth
   09. Hey Mister
   10. Lies Told
   11. Plaything
   12. The End of the Whorl

Here are the different release dates and labels:
31st of january in Germany, Austria and Switzerland, via the German label Minuswelt
15th of january in Northern America, via the Canadian label ArtOfFact
31st of january in Sweden, Scandinavia and the rest of the world, via our Swedish home label, Memento Materia
