We are releasing Lost Tapes: 2002–2009, a collection of rough and raw material that almost no one has heard before. These are songs written by Hans Åkerman between 2002 and 2009 that never made it beyond the demo stage. The promotional photos feature Hans during those years and were taken by the brilliant photographer Jan-Erik Saarinen.
